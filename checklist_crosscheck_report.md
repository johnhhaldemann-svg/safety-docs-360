## Trade-Specific Result

For the stated scope, Structural Steel / Metals - steel erection / decking, the DOCX covers the major high-risk controls well:

- Fall protection, fall rescue, leading edge, connector work, and controlled decking zones.
- Crane picks, hoisting, rigging, qualified rigger/signal person verification, and lift plans.
- Hot work, welding, cutting, fire watch, and extinguishing controls.
- MEWP, ladder, hand tool, laydown, traffic, dropped-object, and access controls.
- Weather, wind, lightning, heat/cold, and restart controls.

Items outside the active scope, such as asbestos handling, lead removal, confined space, respiratory protection, radiation, refrigerants, wastewater operations, FIFRA pesticide work, and RCRA hazardous waste operations, would need to be marked N/A or supplied as separate company programs if Lilly requires the entire checklist package.

## Recommended Determination

Do not submit this DOCX alone as a full checklist pass.

Submit it as the project-specific CSEP plus the required separate uploads, or revise the CSEP package to add explicit gap-close language and attachments for the missing company-level checklist items.

Highest-priority fixes before submission:

1. Add a signed formal company safety policy statement.
2. Add explicit personnel-file and new-hire-orientation language for progressive discipline.
3. Add subcontractor safety representative and subcontractor SDS requirements.
4. Add light-duty / return-to-work policy language.
5. Add fuller drug-free workforce / substance abuse policy language.
6. Add designated occupational clinic/facility information.
7. Add supervisor OSHA 10-hour and management safety-policy training requirements.
8. Attach or upload OSHA 300A logs, full company safety program, actual forms, SDS/chemical inventory, and training records.
